Best Movie

Indiana Jones: Raiders of The Lost Ark

The best movie I've seen recently was Indiana Jones: Raiders of The Lost Ark. What I enjoyed about this movie and the Indiana Jones series as a whole is the suspenseful adventures that Indie gets himself into.

What I liked in particular about the movie Indiana Jones: Raiders of The Lost Ark is the scene at the start of the movie where he replaces the artifact with the bag if sand. This strategy unfortunately fails, setting off one of a booby-trap. This escape scene will always be iconic and memorable.
